Edelweiss Laboratories is a manufacturing facility that provides a contract manufacturing service for the production of creams, ointments and liquids to the cosmetic industry. Speciality categories include galenical dermatologicals, herbal applications utilizing African herbs and oils, and hygiene products such as hand sanitizers and hand cleaners.

Edelweiss Laboratories is an ISO 22716 certified company working under the direct supervision of a pharmacist, two chemical engineers and a team of analytical chemists. We work closely with other experts who can assist you with stability testing and documentation to meet the requirements for EU compliance and to register your product for sale in Europe and the United States of America.

Edelweiss Laboratories provides assistance and insight into the management of contemporary issues such as the formulation of SLES-free, PEG-free, paraben-free and petrolatum-free products as well as the use of natural raw materials for the formulation of your product. In particular Edelweiss offers expert advice on African herbal medicines and essential oils.

PRODUCT DEVELOPMENT

Edelweiss Laboratories provides expert advice in new product development. New products need to be designed so as to ensure that they behave as they are intended to: they must have the required pharmacological affect and they must be are stable under given storage conditions. Edelweiss will develop samples of all products in accordance with the Client's directives. This process may include amongst others the choice of fragrance, the taste profile, the viscosity of semi-solid products, interactions with base materials or other active ingredients and the choice of preservative. Edelweiss will procure the services of top cosmetics formulators where necessary.

Once sample batches have been produced, further laboratory testing is called for. These tests include accelerated stability testing, preservative challenge testing and dissolution testing, with an option for product assay. We highly recommend preservative challenge testing for all liquids and semi-solids and refer these procedures to the SABS-recommended Merieux Laboratories for execution and certification. Procedures that are currently sub-contracted are HPLC and Gas Chromatography.

While samples are being tested and proved suitable for production, packaging is chosen for marketing aesthetics as well product stability. Edelweiss keeps a range of 'off-the-shelf' packaging and will point the client in the appropriate direction for a wider range.
